Introduction to Deep Learning

This collection is meant for newcomers to the field of Deep Learning. While a general understanding of Machine Learning as a whole would be beneficial, the first post presented will provide a review of the necessary concepts. If you can explain the regression and classification tasks, understand the role of convex-optimization in learning algorithms, and know the difference between overfitting and underfitting then you can go ahead and skip it.

The second post will introduce you to setting up a python development environment for PyTorch. Python is the defacto language in machine learning when it comes to research and prototyping. While it obviously is not the fastest language in existence, its simple syntax, combined with powerful C++ backed libraries like PyTorch and Tensorflow make it quite flexible.

The remaining posts will present the various types of neural networks that are widely used today. They are by no means an exhaustive account of every neural network architecture that exists as the field is ever changing, but they will provide a good overview of the basics.
